# Aurelia Words Privacy Policy Draft

Last updated: April 23, 2026

Aurelia Words is designed to work without an account and without sending gameplay data to a remote service.

## What the app stores
- Game progress for daily and practice rounds
- Local settings such as haptics, color-blind mode, and reduced motion
- Local statistics such as wins, streaks, and guess distribution

## What the app does not collect in this version
- No account information
- No analytics
- No advertising identifiers
- No contact list, camera, microphone, or location data
- No third-party SDK tracking

## How data is used
All saved information is used only to power local gameplay, accessibility preferences, and statistics on the device.
