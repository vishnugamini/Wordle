## Full Description Draft
Aurelia Words is a daily word puzzle with unlimited practice and a polished, theatrical presentation.

Play one featured puzzle each day in Daily mode, then keep going in Practice mode with fresh local rounds whenever you want more. Every guess is paired with rich tile feedback, clear keyboard states, and a design that feels elegant without getting in the way.

Version 1 focuses on a smooth iPhone experience with:
- daily play
- unlimited practice
- local statistics
- color-blind palette support
- reduced motion support
- no account required
